macaroni and cheese

description

ingredients

  • 8 oz (about 225g) elbow macaroni or any pasta shape you prefer
  • 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Cooking spray or extra butter for greasing the baking dish

steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, melt the butter over medium heat.
  4. Once the butter is melted, add the flour and whisk constantly until smooth and bubbly, about 2 minutes.
  5. Gradually pour in the milk while whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
  6. Continue to cook and whisk until the mixture thickens, about 5 minutes.
  7. Add the shredded cheddar cheese and mozzarella cheese to the sauce, stirring until melted and smooth.
  8. Season the cheese sauce with salt, black pepper, and paprika if using.
  9. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir until well coated.
  10. Pour the macaroni and cheese mixture into the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.
  11. If desired, sprinkle breadcrumbs evenly over the top of the macaroni and cheese for a crunchy topping.
  12. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the edges are bubbly.
  13. Let the mac and cheese cool for a few minutes before serving.
