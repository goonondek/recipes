green spaghetti

description

ingredients

  • 12 oz (about 340g) spaghetti
  • 4 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor or blender, combine the fresh spinach leaves, basil leaves, minced garlic, pine nuts (if using), grated Parmesan cheese, and olive oil.
  3. Blend the ingredients until smooth, adding more olive oil if necessary to reach your desired consistency. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a large skillet, heat the green sauce over medium heat until warmed through, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the green sauce, tossing gently to coat the pasta evenly.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the spaghetti to absorb the flavors of the sauce.
  7. Serve the green spaghetti hot, garnished with additional grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
